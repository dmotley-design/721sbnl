Our Story

Welcome to 721 Sports Bar & Lounge

At 721, we believe that "sports bar food" should be more than an afterthought. We've redefined the game day experience by combining the high-energy atmosphere of a lounge with a culinary commitment usually reserved for fine dining. Our mission is simple: to provide an elevated sports dining experience where quality is never sacrificed for the score.

The House-Made Standard

What truly sets us apart is our obsession with the details. We don't believe in shortcuts, and we definitely don't believe in "out of a box."

  • The Kitchen: Every sauce, seasoning, and marinade is crafted right here in our kitchen. Our signature wings are slow-smoked to perfection, and our meats are marinated with precision. We even go as far as the humble pickle—we don't buy them; we buy fresh cucumbers and brine them in-house to ensure that every crunch is earned.
  • The Bar: Our commitment to craft extends to your glass. You won't find pre-mixed syrups here. All of our drink mixes and syrups are made in-house, ensuring that every cocktail is as vibrant and fresh as the ingredients we source.

Simple Roots, Elevated Choices

The beauty of 721 is the power of choice. We designed our menu to cater to exactly how you feel in the moment.

If you're looking for the classic comfort of a juicy burger, crispy fries, and a cold domestic beer, we do the classics better than anyone. But if you're looking to level up your evening with a perfectly seared filet and a hand-crafted Old Fashioned, the option is always there.

Whether you're here for the big game, a casual happy hour, or a celebratory dinner, 721 offers a simple dining experience with the luxury of more.
